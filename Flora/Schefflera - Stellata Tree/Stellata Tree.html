DescriptionSmall trees or apparently straggling shrubs. Leaves compound, digitate, alternate, spiral, pulvinate, stipule adnate to the petiole; petioles 6-20 cm long, sheathing at base; petiolule 2-6 cm long; leaflets 3-6, usually 5, lamina 4.2-10 x 3.5-6.4 cm, obovate, sometimes oblique, apex rounded to slightly obtuse or emarginate, base attenuate, glabrous, shining above, margin recurved; midrib impressed above; secondary_nerves 4-6 pairs, looped; tertiary_nerves broadly reticulate. Flowers in umbels arranged in panicled racemes, 5-8 per umbel. Drupe, subglobose, 0.4-0.5 cm diameter; style tip persistent in fruits; seeds compressed.
