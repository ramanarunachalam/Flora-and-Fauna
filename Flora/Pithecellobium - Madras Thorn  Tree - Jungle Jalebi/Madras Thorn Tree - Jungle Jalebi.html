DescriptionLarge, nearly evergreen tree that grows up to 20 m or more in height, Madras Thorn has a broad crown (to 30 m across) and a short trunk (to 1 m thick) At the base of each leaf is normally found a pair of short, sharp spines, though some specimens are spineless. Leaves are deciduous but foliage is persistent, as the new leaves appear while the old ones are being shed
