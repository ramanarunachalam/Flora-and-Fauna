DescriptionMedium sized tree, reaching from 30-60 feet (9-18 m) tall. The trunk is smooth and greenish gray, and often swollen at the base. The alternate leaves are 6-11 inches (15-28 cm) in length and compound, with 5-9 leaflets. The large and showy flowers are perfect and terminal, with a long peduncle. The petals and numerous stamens are white. The fruit is a smooth, green capsule, 4-8 inches (10-20 cm) in length, and splits opens naturally on longitudinal sutures when ripe. The seeds are rounded but irregular, with about 10-25 per fruit. They are about 1 inch (2.5 cm) in diameter, with a light brown testa.
