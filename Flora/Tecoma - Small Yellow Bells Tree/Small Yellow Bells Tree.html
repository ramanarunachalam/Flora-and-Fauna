DescriptionYellow trumpet flower is a variety of Tecoma stans, the Yellow Bells. It has narrower and lacy leaflets which are more deeply toothed. The name angustata means narrowed. This is a shrub or small tree that reaches 15-20 feet in height and can be pruned heavily after flowering to keep it compact. The fruit on is a "bean type" capsule about 8 inches long that ripens to a chocolate brown color.
