DescriptionPimenta dioica is a slow growing evergreen tree that grows to a height of about 9 m (about 30 ft), with whitish gray bark that peels in thin sheets. Allspice as a name is due to the fact that the leaves and fruit smell like a combination of cloves, black pepper, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
