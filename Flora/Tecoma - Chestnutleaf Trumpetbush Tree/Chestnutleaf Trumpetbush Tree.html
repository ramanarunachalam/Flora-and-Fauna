DescriptionThis is a close cousin of Yellow Bells, but there is a big difference - Yellow Bells has compound leaves, whereas this one has simple leaves. In the Yellow Bells, the clusters of flowers bend the branch and are commonly seen beautifully hanging down. In Chestnutleaf Trumpetbush, clusters of yellow bell-shaped flowers appear at the end of branches which spread outwards. The leaves have serrated margins.
